Nostalgic Brilliance with Soulful Feelings

Shyamal Mitra, a Naihati-born maestro of Bengali music, enchanted audiences with his mellifluous voice and timeless compositions. Renowned for versatility, he crafted soul-stirring melodies across genres, leaving an indelible mark in Indian music. His prolific career spanned decades, endearing him as an iconic figure rooted in Naihati's musical heritage.
